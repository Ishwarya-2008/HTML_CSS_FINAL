    • Our expert-led care
    team

    At Headspace, we understand that mental health is a lifelong
    journey. In order to meet you where you are, our care team
    consists of mental health experts committed to delivering you
    the right care at the right time.


    Combined with self-guided resources like
    meditation and mindfulness exercises and expert-vetted
    articles, our care team is here to help you reach and maintain
    your personal mental health goals.

    Your mental health team

    Headspace's goal is to make meditation, mindfulness, and mental health as simple, compassionate,
    and accessible as possible for everyone.


    Our certified meditation and mindfulness teachers have trained with some of the world's top experts.
    They've received training through programs like Mindfulness Based Stress Reduction and/or have
    completed 200 hours of Mindfulness Meditation teacher training. All of our mental health coaches are
    nationally board-certified by an accredited institution, or have earned a Master's degree in a
    psychology related field. From daily stress and anxiety to support through big life events, your coach
    will draw from clinically-validated tools like CBT, ACT, and other well-being practices tailored to
    deliver safe, effective, and timely care.


    Headspace's culturally-responsive care team reflects the diversity of our users, ensuring that everyone
    feels not only seen but a sense of belonging. Get to know some of our team below.
