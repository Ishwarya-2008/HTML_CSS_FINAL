About Headspace

Headspace's mission is to provide every person access to lifelong mental health support.

Who we are

Think of Headspace as your everyday mental health companion, making it easy to put your mind first with personalized support — from self-guided to human-delivered clinical care.

What we do

Through evidence-based meditation and mindfulness tools, coaching, therapy and psychiatry, Headspace helps you create life-changing habits to support your mental health.

How we do it

Our values guide everything: Make the Mission Matter, Iterate to Great, Own the Outcome, Connect with Courage — applied across product, clinical care and research.

Headspace

We make mental health support accessible to everyone, no matter background or experience. Our app delivers meditations, sleep content, movement, coaching and therapy — designed to fit into everyday life. We partner with employers and organizations to extend mental health care through coaching, therapy, psychiatry and EAP services.

Our team includes clinicians, producers and data scientists working to help millions be healthier and more productive.
